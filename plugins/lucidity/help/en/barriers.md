# Barriers

Players may choose to bolster the security of their rooms. They can do this incrementally. Doing so creates a multiplicative effect on Lucidity costs for things like imposing illusions, bridge tolls, and so on.

One cannot strengthen the barriers of other people's rooms

## Commands

`barrier/strengthen` -- increases the Barrier in the room you're in

## Acceptable Use

There are no restrictions on use of this command at this time.
